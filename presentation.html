Reasearch Questions

  • Does the use of multiple stimuli increase the sensation of immersion?
  • Can we influence the emotions of users?

Goals

  • Create a software and hardware framework that can be used for future research
  • Addition of vibration, temperature, and EMS actuation to a VR device's face cushion
Related Work

Haptic Feedback

  • high precision with recognation rates of 96.4%, Oliver Beren Kaul and Micael Rohs
  • phantom sensation at vibrator distances below two point threshold, Kerdegari et al. and Lee et al.
  • sensation of a false heartbeat can influence emotions, Narihiro Nishimura et al.
Source: Oliver Beren Kaul and Micael Rohs, Haptichead, CHI 2017

Temperature Feedback

  • Using temperature actuation increases level of immersion
  • Aceptable acuracy, with directional queues and five actuators
  • Use of moving patterns possible
Source: Roshan Peiris, Wei Peng, Zikun Chen, et al., ThermoVR, CHI 2017

EMS Feedback

  • TreeSense creates sensations of branches growing and birds landing on them felt through electrodes on arms
  • EMS often used for muscle contraction, i.e., muscle movement
Source: https://www.media.mit.edu/projects/treesense/
